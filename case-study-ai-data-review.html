What was broken

Every loan required two rounds of manual effort from 2 team members 7 hours total reviewing hundreds of data points against legal documents, plus 24 hours wait time on vendor. It can never be done on time, and business can never scale.

Limited capability of current legacy system and human error pose risk to data accuracy.

How I designed the new solution to hit the goals

As the sole designer, I redesigned the experience to move from fully manual review to AI-driven exception-based auditing.

Efficiency gain

Mapping data to their original document coordinates addressed user's biggest time spent during review

Trust in AI

Explainable AI and confidence-based filter allows users to bypass verified data and focus on uncertain answers

Overhead reduction

Redefined operational workflow from 2-person double check to single-user oversight

Increased accuracy

Limit manual touchpoint reduced human error, and robust real time data validation prevented unverified data getting into system

Cost saving

In-house AI solution cut the millions of vendor cost
